Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before your first ride.

Where can I ride and park?

Ride on bike lanes or streets where permitted. Park in designated zones shown in the app; never block sidewalks or doorways.

Do I need a helmet?

We strongly recommend helmets for every rider. Local regulations may require them—check your city rules.

How fast do vehicles go?

Top speed varies by city, usually 15–20 mph (25–32 km/h) with slow zones near busy areas.

What happens if a vehicle is damaged?

Use the in‑app report tool to flag any issues. Our ops team will pause the vehicle and repair or swap it.
